Admissions 2025–26

Everything you need to know about joining Rajalakshmi Engineering College.

01

Apply Online

Fill the online application form on the official REC website or visit the admissions office with required documents.

02

Eligibility Check

UG: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Maths. TNEA counselling rank accepted. Management quota also available.

03

Document Verification

Submit certificates, mark sheets, community certificate, and passport photos at the admissions counter.

04

Confirmation & Fee

Pay the semester fee to confirm your seat. Scholarship options available for eligible students.
