Who We Are

About Rajalakshmi Engineering College

Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC) is a premier private engineering institution in Thandalam, Sriperumbudur, Chennai. Founded in 1997 by the Rajalakshmi Educational Trust, REC is affiliated with Anna University and spread across a lush 23-acre green campus.

With over 5,000 students, 500+ faculty members, and 25+ programs from UG to PhD, REC has earned its place among Tamil Nadu's most respected engineering institutions — consistently achieving top placement records and national academic rankings.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognised centre of engineering education, nurturing innovation and producing industry-ready, socially responsible professionals.

Our Mission

Deliver quality technical education, promote cutting-edge research, build strong industry alliances, and cultivate ethical, skilled engineers who create societal value.

Innovation Hub

REC !dea Factory

REC's flagship !dea Factory is an AICTE-approved Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Centre — a stunning multi-level maker-space with a glass skylight atrium, coloured panel floors, and open collaboration zones where students turn bold ideas into real products.

From IoT prototypes to funded startups, the !dea Factory provides every resource a young innovator needs under one architecturally iconic roof.

  • IoT Development Kits & Embedded Systems
  • 3D Printers, Laser Cutters & Fabrication Tools
  • AI / ML Workstations & Data Science Rigs
  • Startup Incubation & Seed Funding Support
  • Industry Mentorship & Expert Workshops
  • Multi-level Glass-Skylight Collaboration Atrium
